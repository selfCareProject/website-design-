Our Device:

In order to improve life quality for patients, we adapted a newly designed device that helps patients to monitor their health and get more connected with their doctors.

Self-care is a system ,In which you can measure your temperature, heart rate & pressure, In case of serious conditions it alerts your doctor & relatives with an SMS.

How to use it:

Self care device is easy to use :

  1. You need to install the application to your android device. Install



  2. Power On the device.


  3. Decide what you want to measure.


  4. The result will appear on your phone screen And saved in
    your profile.


  5. In the same time a copy from your measurements will be sent
    to your doctor.


  6. You can see charts for your measurements for more details
    by clicking on the icons of temperature, pressure & heart beat.
